Who We Are

SZTN Trading & Projects is a professional security and automation solutions company dedicated to protecting lives, property, and assets through technology-driven systems.

Unlike many contractors, we are both suppliers and installers, allowing us to offer competitive pricing, faster turnaround times, and complete technical accountability from start to finish. We deliver complete solutions that combine surveillance, access control, electric fencing, and solar backup power.
